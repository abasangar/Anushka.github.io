Strengths

  • Strong 19.6% revenue growth from 2022–2024
  • Improved inventory management (turnover 8.59× → 10.37×)
  • Large global presence in 120+ countries
  • Improving gross profit margin (17.8% → 19.4%)

Weaknesses

  • Net profit margin compression (6.34% → 3.21%)
  • High financial leverage & growing long-term debt
  • Falling ROA (3.71% → 2.15%)
  • Equity investment losses in 2024

Opportunities

  • Ultium EV platform investments
  • Cost optimization & automation initiatives
  • Hyundai strategic partnership
  • Growing autonomous vehicle demand

Threats

  • Macroeconomic risks & high interest rates
  • Supply chain vulnerability (5,700+ suppliers)
  • Regulatory & compliance pressures
  • EV competition from Tesla & legacy OEMs
